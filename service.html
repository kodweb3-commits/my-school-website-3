FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for membership?

Registration is fully online — visit our homepage, fill in your details, verify your email, then collect your membership card from the front desk on your first visit.

Can I access the digital library from home?

Yes! The digital library is available 24/7 from any device. Log in with your member credentials on our portal to access e-books, audiobooks, and databases from anywhere.

What happens if I return a book late?

Students get a 7-day grace period at no cost. General members are charged ₦50 per book per day for late returns. You can renew online up to 2 times to avoid late fees.

How do I book a private study room?

Study rooms can be booked online through the member portal, by phone, or at the front desk. Rooms are available in 2-hour blocks and can be reserved up to 7 days in advance.

Is the library open on public holidays?

Yes, we operate on public holidays with reduced hours of 10:00am – 4:00pm. Some specialist services may be unavailable. Check our contact page for the latest schedule.

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! Students with a valid university or college ID receive 30% off the Member plan and 20% off the Scholar plan. Bring your ID to the front desk to activate your discount.
