Visit the Library

We're centrally located in Victoria Island — easily accessible by bus, BRT, and car.

Getting Here

By Bus / BRTTake the BRT to Victoria Island terminal. 5-minute walk from Eko Hotel Bus Stop on Adetokunbo Ademola Street.
By CarEnter from Akin Adesola Street heading south. Turn left onto Knowledge Avenue. Free parking available in our compound.
By Okada / KekeAsk for "Sky-High Library, Knowledge Avenue." Most local riders know the landmark. 10-min ride from CMS.
By FerryAlight at the VI Ferry Terminal and take a 10-minute walk or keke ride to Knowledge Avenue.
